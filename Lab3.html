Welcome to Joe’s Website

Joe Casabona is an accredited college course developer and professor.

He also has his Master’s Degree in Software Engineering, is a Front End Developer, and hosts multiple podcasts.

Joe started his career almost 20 years ago as a freelance web developer and has has worked on projects for Disney, National Geographic, The Boys & Girls Clubs of America, and more. He also teaches Computer Science at The University of Scranton, produces training videos, and authors books and courses on:

  • WordPress
  • HTML and CSS
  • Podcasting

When he’s not creating courses or writing books, you can find Joe spending time with his family, enjoying baseball or watching Star Wars.
